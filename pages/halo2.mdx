
Halo 2 is the latest proving system used by Zcash.
It does not require a trusted setup.
and has several optimizations
over [Plonk](/halo2/plonk)

The following sections are an
[introduction to Proving Systems](/halo2/intro). 

Proving Systems are rather complex and
it is not easy to describe how they work
without a certain amount of mathematics.
However, we will try to keep that to a minimum.
